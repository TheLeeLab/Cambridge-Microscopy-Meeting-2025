The Cambridge Microscopy Meeting 2025 (CMM2025) is a dedicated forum organised by and for microscopy developers in and around Cambridge. The meeting will feature talks and posters, highlighting advancements in microscope development and applications. It will be held on 7th–8th Apr 2025 at the Yusuf Hamied Department of Chemistry at the University of Cambridge. Registration for the meeting is required. Thanks to our amazing sponsors, only a nominal registration fee will be charged.

Important dates

Abstract submission opens Dec 2024
Abstract submission closes Feb 2025 (23:59 BST)
Programme announced Mar 2025
Meeting dates 7-8 Apr 2023

Location

The meeting will be held in the Bristol-Myers Squibb (BMS) lecture theatre at the Yusuf Hamied Department of Chemistry. You can access the lecture theatre from the entrance of the department that is closest to the Scott Polar Research Institute (Polar Museum).
